Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

One Line a Day ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information. If you have questions, contact us at support@onelineaday.app.

What We Collect

We believe your personal journals belong to you alone. The App collects the following data for the sole purpose of providing its core functionality:

  • Journal Entries: Text you write in the App.
  • Voice Audio: Optional audio captured for transcription purposes.

Storage: All your data is stored ONLY on your device and synced via your personal Apple iCloud account. We do not operate any developer-side servers and have zero access to your data.

iCloud Sync

Your data is synced across your devices using Apple's iCloud service. This synchronization is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We cannot access any data stored in your iCloud account.

Voice & Speech Recognition

If you use the voice-to-text feature, the App uses Apple's on-device Speech Recognition technology. Your audio is processed locally or via Apple's servers (as per your system settings), but it is never stored or accessed by the developer.

Purchases

All in-app purchases are handled by Apple. We never see or store your payment information, credit card numbers, or billing address. Transactions are governed by the Apple Media Services Terms and Conditions.

Analytics, Ads, and Tracking

One Line a Day is a clean, private experience. We use no analytics SDKs, no advertising networks, and no tracking cookies. We do not sell your data, and we never will.

GDPR & Data Rights

We support the rights of users under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we do not store your data on our servers, you can exercise your rights directly:

  • Access & Export: You can view and export your data at any time via the in-app tools.
  • Deletion: You can delete your entries within the app or by deleting the app and its associated data from your device and iCloud.

CCPA Compliance

We do not sell personal information as defined by the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
