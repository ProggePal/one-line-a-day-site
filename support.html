Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export my entries?

Go to the Profile tab, tap Export. You can copy the last 7 days, this week, this month, or all entries. For large exports, use the Share option to send as a text file via email or Files.

Can I edit a past entry?

Entries are intentionally read-only after the day ends. This is by design — it preserves the honest, in-the-moment nature of the journal.

How does iCloud sync work?

Your entries sync automatically across all your iPhones and iPads signed into the same iCloud account. No account needed — it just works.

How do I delete all my data?

Go to Profile → scroll to the bottom → tap Delete All Data. This permanently removes all entries from your device and iCloud. This cannot be undone.

How do I restore my voice transcription purchase?

Go to Profile → tap Restore Purchases. Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device signed with the same Apple ID.

The voice transcription isn't working. What do I do?

Make sure you've granted microphone and speech recognition permissions in Settings → Privacy. If the issue persists, contact us below.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your entries are stored only on your device and in your personal iCloud. We have no servers, no accounts, and never see your data. Read our full Privacy Policy.
